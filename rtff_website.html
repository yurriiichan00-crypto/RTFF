Proven Trading Strategies

📊 Risk Management Mastery

Position Sizing: The most crucial aspect. Control quantity, not stop loss.

Stop Loss Strategy: Decide before entering. Use logical, not arbitrary stop losses.

Mathematical Edge: Even 10% win rate with proper R-factor can be profitable.

🎯 Nifty: 30-Min Opening Range Breakout

Time Required: Maximum 35 minutes per day

Setup: Break above previous day's high for long positions

Market Type: Effective in trending markets

📈 VWAP Strategy for Sideways Markets

Market Coverage: Works for 70% of trading days (sideways markets)

Indicator: VWAP with Bands 2 and 3

Edge: Capitalizes on range-bound market movements

🪙 Bitcoin: Tokyo/London Session Breakout

Setup: Narrow range in Tokyo session

Entry: 1-hour candle close outside the session box

Targets: 1x box size for first, 1.5x for second target

⏰ Patience: "Waiting is Trading"

Key Principle: Don't force trades when markets aren't clear

Market Reality: Choppy markets can last hours

Capital Rule: Less capital = more patience required

📊 Option Selling vs. Buying

Option Selling: 66% natural success probability

Option Buying: Only 33% success rate, requires fast moves

Recommendation: Option buying not for beginners
